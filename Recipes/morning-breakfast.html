Morning Breakfast Recipe

Description

When I wake up and want to be lazy, this is the recipe that gets me everything I need: Caffine, fiber, and protein.

Ingredients

Coffee

  • 20-30 grams of Coffee Beans
  • 1 Chemex Filter
  • Boiling Water

Oatmeal

  • 40 grams of Oatmeal
  • 1 tbsp of Chia Seeds
  • Maple Syrup
  • Salt

Directions

Coffee

  1. Boil a kettle of coffee
  2. Grind coffee beans (10g per cup)
  3. Place coffee grounds in chemex filter
  4. Pour over boiled water over coffee grounds (16 oz per gram of coffee)

Oatmeal

  1. Mix oatmeal and chia seeds together
  2. Pour boiling water over oatmeal until just covered
  3. Place plate over bowl to steam
  4. Once coffee is ready, the oatmeal will be ready
  5. Add salt and maple syrup
