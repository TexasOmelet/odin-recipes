Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a rustic and hardy italian recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Ground Beef or Turkey
  • 1 Medium Sized Onion
  • 2 Diced Carrots
  • 2 Celery Stalks
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tsp of Chili Flakes
  • 2 Sticks of Basil
  • 1 Can of Whole Peeled Tomatos
  • 1 Pack of Spaghetti
  • Salt to Taste
  • Pepper to Taste

Directions

Sear Ground Beef

  1. Turn stove to medium high
  2. Sear ground beef
  3. Take the ground beef out of the pot and save for later

Sautee Vegetables

  1. Add a neutral oil
  2. Add diced onions, carrots, and celery
  3. Add salt
  4. Cover pot and stir occassionaly until vegetables are soft

Sautee Aromatics

  1. Reduce heat to medium low
  2. Push sauteed vegetables to one half of the pan
  3. Add oil to the other half of the pan
  4. Brown garlic, basil, and chili pepper flakes

Simmer Tomato Sauce

  1. Add ground beef back to pot and stir together
  2. Crush whole peeled tomatos and add liquids to pot
  3. Add half tomato can of water
  4. Simmer until Reduced

Boil Pasta

  1. In another pot, boil heavily salted water
  2. Add pasta and cook until aldente

Combine Pasta and Bologenese

  1. Add two ladles of pasta water into the Bologenese
  2. Add spaghetti
  3. Reduce until desired consistency

Finish Bologenese

  1. Kill the heat
  2. Grate parmigiano Reggiano and stir into Bologenese
