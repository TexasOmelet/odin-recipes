Black Beans Recipe

Description

Hardy black beans recipe. Great side for rice and chicken. Amazing source of fiber and protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Celery Stalks
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 3 Slices of Bacon
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Cans of Black Beans

Directions

Cook Bacon and Sautee Vegetables

  1. Stove on medium-high
  2. Cook 3 bacon slices
  3. Sautee onions, carrots, celery, bell pepper
  4. Cover pot and stir occasionally

Cook Aromatics

  1. Lower heat to medium low
  2. Push vegetables to other half of pot
  3. Brown garlic and add spices: chili powder, paprika, cayenne pepper
  4. Stir in tomato paste

Simmer beans

  1. Add 2 cans of black beans
  2. Add one full can of water
  3. Simmer until thick
